FromEleventh Bde

Time11.40 pm
TO: 4th Div
Sender's Number: B.M. 3 Day of Month: Thirty first In reply to Number:

On visiting the outpost line from St. SAUVEUR - MALINAUX - HOURPARI I found the 4th Middlesex Regt had taken up outposts on this line intermingled with the E. LAN. Regt which already held it. I instructed the Middlesex Regt to withdraw their groups and form a support to the E. LAN pending further instructions. As placed the two lines of outposts were a serious menace to each other.

Operation Order No 1

Appendix to the Op order No 1 of 31 August

31.8.14

1.(a)  The French met with great success in the neighbourhood of GUISE, where the German Guard & 10th Corp were driven back to the OISE. The French forces were also engaged yesterday in the direction of PERONNE.

b)  Our Cavalry are holding the OISE south of COMPIEGNE. The Secornd Army Corps advances on our left.

2.  The Fourth Div. will move west in two columns today, as shown in the march table attached. Each column will find a small rear guard.

3.  All sick will be evacuated by supply secns of train to refilling point.

4.  Railhead & supply rendezvous for supply column - LE PLESSIS BELLVILLE (15 miles S.W. of CREPY).

5.  Refilling point will be on fork of roads at first V of VILLENEUVE.

6.  12th Fd Ambce will detail medical officer, 3 Fd ambces & necessay personell to accompany right column & march in rear. 10th Fd Ambce will detail medical officer & 1 Fd ambce and necessary personell to accompany left column & march in rear.

[page torn] Reports to head of left column.

[Signed]
