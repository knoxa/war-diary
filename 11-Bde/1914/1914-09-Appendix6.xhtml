11th Infantry Brigade, War Diary, September 1914

Appendix 6

TO: Brig Gen Hunter Weston.
Sender's Number: SH 41 Day of Month: First In reply to Number:

It is not proposed that the outposts found by the troops in various areas should link up. You will therefore be responsible for the protection of the whole perimeter of your area. The area at FRESNOY and the combined areas of BARON & VERSIGNY are each providing their own protection. AAA Divn HdQrs is at BARON.


From4. Div.
PlaceBARON
Time6 pm
TO: Eleventh Brigade.
Sender's Number: G 37 Day of Month: First In reply to Number:

Reference my G26 Innis. Fus. now at RARAY are quite happy. Report that they were in difficulties incorrect aaa You can now therefore retire by a shorter way aaa You are to act as rearguard to the force till you reach the line MT CARNON - RARAY when rearguard duties will be taken over by 12 & 19 Brigades and you will march and take up position at FRESNOY LE LUST. 10th Brig evacuating wounded & captured German guns from NERY. Get in communication with them aaa They have been ordered to retire through 19 & 12 Brigs on the line DUCY - BOASNES. Innis Fus have been ordered back to BEALIEU LE NEUF where they will rejoin the Brigade. 12 and 19 Brigs as soon as they have covered retirement of 10 & 11 Brigs will retire to BEAULIEU LE NEUF and MONTEPILLOY respectively.


From4. Div.
PlaceRARAY
Time12.45pm
TO: Eleventh Brigade.
Sender's Number: G 26 Day of Month: First In reply to Number:

Orders received from Corps HdQrs that we are to retire slowly first on line Mt CORNON - CHAMICY then on FRESNOY LE LUST - MONTEPILLOY aaa General situation 9.30 am - 12th Brig Mt CORNON, 10th Brig between SUCITERIE DE NERY and LA BOUSSIERE Fe with one battn at NERY. 19th Brig FAY Fe helping BRIGGS who is said to be engaged with Germans at VANICELLES. aaa Inniskilling detatchde from 12th Brig are about ST VAAST. They have been shelled in VERBERIE and had better come under you. This is your authority to take them over for the present. Gen wishes you to retire approximately on the line DE LONGMONT point 126 RARAY - CHAMICY withdrawing the Inniskillings as you pass ST VAAST and letting 19th Brig know what you are doing if possible. Latter should retire by FAY Fe LA BARDE Fe - RULLY keeping touch with you and 10th Brig. If you can get in touch with them pass this message on at once. Div H.Q. will move at 10 am to North end RULLY.


FromFourth Division
PlacePoint 126
Time9.45pm
