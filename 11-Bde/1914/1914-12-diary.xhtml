1st Dec.   4.5 am
Pt 20 Farm

Situation reported normal.

 B.M.1, B.M.13 App 86
6.50 pm

Shelling.

Somerset sectionnil
Rif Brig sectionnil
Hants sectionnil
E Lan section8 small shells on left trench about 9.15 am. None blind.

Work.

Somerset sectionRight trench reconstructed owing to flooding.
Rif Brig sectionBreastwork in firing line improved. Supporting trenches for Hants continued. Wire put out.
Hants sectionMain trench improved. facings made, faggots collected.
E Lan sectionWork on 2nd line trench & communication trench to Convent. Fire trenches deepened & cleaned.

200 men work on main Breastwork line

new German trench dug south of south face of left trench of Somersets.

Following reinforcements joined night of 30th Nov. Somersets 1 officer, 50 O.R. E Lan 1 officers 154 O.R. Rif Brig. 25 O.R.

Hour, Date, Place

Summary of Events and Information

Remarks
2nd Dec.   4 am
Pt 20 Farm

Situation reported normal.

 B.M.1, B.M.3 App 87
9.5 am

Somersets report work on new German trench stopped. Mostly demolished by shell fire yesterday.
6.25 pm

Shelling. More than usual.

Somerset sectionAbout 2 heavy howitzers & 4 shrapnel an hour from WARNETON & DEULMONT directions. None blind.
Rif Brig section5 white holes near trenches. 2 blind in front.
Hants sectionSmall gun fired 12 shells about 10 a.m. at end of main trench by tunnel. 2 blind.
E Lan section10 heavy shells at Convent about 10 a.m. & again at 2 p.m. 10 more. 2 blind. 10 Little Willies at Burnt Fm in front of Convent. 12 large shells over left trench about 10 a.m.

Work.

150 men in two reliefs in Breastwork line. London RB on 2nd line.

Somerset sectionCommunication trench dug. Latrines & drainage improved.
Rif Brig sectionConstruction of overhead cover & obstacles continued.
Hants sectionWiring & obstacles continued. Communications trenches deepened etc.
E Lan sectionWork on 2nd line trench & communication trenches continued also fallen in trenches revetted.

No movement except reoccupation of GERMAN HOUSE.

HIS MAJESTY visited NIEPPE & inspected detachments of the Brigade.
