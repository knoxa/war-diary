11th Infantry Brigade, War Diary, August 1914

Appendix 3

TO: HQ 11th Inf Brig
Sender's Number: GGG 8 Day of Month: Twentysixth In reply to Number:

If you have not already done so you should get your brigade clear of the area of FONTAINE-AU-PIRE AAA The Division is to take up the line CAUDRY (eastern side) - CARRIERE - MONS D-ESNES AAA Your front will be from about LE COQUELEFS to the railway bridge South of FONTAINE AAA You should begin constructing this position as soon as possible AAA Get in touch with CAUDRY towards IIIrd Div AAA Div H Qrs remain at HAUCOURT CH. AAA Charge remainder of your brigade to rendezvous at 8 am just South of Railway line on road CARRIERES - LIGNY.


FromFourth Division
Place [unclear]
Time5.30 am
TO: HANTS - RIF BRIG - E. LANCS - SOM. L.I.
Sender's Number: Bm 1 Day of Month: Twentyninth In reply to Number:

Information:-

The 11th I.B. hdqrs & remainder of the brigade arrive at LESCLOYES some time tonight. AAA The bridges over the river OISE at PONT-L-EVEQUE and OURSCAMP are guarded by detatchments R.E., and MACHINE GUNS AAA Small posts have been thrown out round LESCLOYES watching all approaches from the North on a line running through OURSCAMP on the W, to junction roads south of M in MAUPAS at the E exit of CARLEPONT. AAA Detachments to each consisting of 25 men and 1 Officer will be posted at 10 a.m. by the HANTS RGT. AAA The Irish Horse will patrol on the E bank of the river OISE from BAILLY on the S to OURSCAMP from daybreak on the 30th AUG. AAA DIV Hd Qrds are at the MAIRIE at CARLEPONT AAA.

All details from other units and divisions will be attached to the Twelfth Inf Brig. AAA. The HANTS RGT. AAA. Each unit will provide 2 orderlies for 11th I.B. hdqrs. One of these orderlies on arrival at H.Q. will return to his unit and ascertain the exact position of his C.O.s hdqrs.


FromELEVENTH INF BRIG
PlaceS. house but one in the village of LES CLOYES
Time9.40 am
